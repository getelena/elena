Become an Elena sponsor ​

Elena is an MIT-licensed open source project and completely free to use. However, we understand that the project won’t remain sustainable forever without financial backing. Hence, we’re planning to set up sponsorship at Open Collective later.

If you have questions regarding the sponsorship, please reach out to hi@elenajs.com.
